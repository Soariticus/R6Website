What is Rainbow Six Siege?

Rainbow Six: Siege is a tactical shooter. Imagine something like Call of Duty, but with realistic graphics, realistic gameplay, and realistic mechanics; thinking about it, Rainbow Six: Siege is absolutely nothing like Call of Duty. Simply put, it is a much superior game. The entire game is based around 2 teams, an attacking and a defending team. There are 3 gamemodes, secure area, hostage and lastly, the most played and most fun one, bomb.
There is a huge amount of operators (characters) to pick from, my personal favorites are Zofia, the Russian attacking operator, and Jager, the German defending operator

The Community

To be fair, the community isn't the best. There are some great teachers on youtube, and a couple of really cool people on it. However, sadly, most of the community is incredibly toxic. Writing "ez" after a game, calling people shit if they can't win a 1v5, etc. It's absolutely ridiculous honestly. Apart from that, I have made some really good friends on here and had a blast playing with them.

Getting New Operators (Characters)

Earning new operators is a bit of a chore, you earn this currency every match, you need 25.000 for a DLC op, which are the most interesting ones generally. On average this will take about 10-ish hours to earn together, which isn't too bad, but with the amount of operators in the game, it's quite a chore to get all of them.
You can also buy currency, which is of a different type and you can buy certain cosmetics with, one operator will set you back around 5 euro, which is fair to me.
